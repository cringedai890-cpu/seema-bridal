Booking Policies & FAQs

Do you travel for destination weddings?

Yes, Seema and her senior team travel directly to venues globally for destination weddings to provide on-site services.

Can I get a makeup trial before booking?

Absolutely. We offer paid trials to test out custom looks, evaluate skin tones, and check product compatibility before your big day.

What are your payment terms?

We require a partial advance booking amount to secure your date. The remaining balance is due 100% on the event date (Cash and digital transfers accepted).

What budget kits do you use for party bookings?

For standard/party bookings, we use highly reliable brands including Sugar, Nykaa, Maybelline, Makeup Revolution, and Faces.
